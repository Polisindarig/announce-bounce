FAQ

Common questions.

Why buy on MEXC instead of Binance?

When Binance announces a listing, the coin isn't tradeable there yet — but it's often already on MEXC. We buy on MEXC to capture the anticipatory pump before Binance opens trading.

How fast does the bot react?

Under 5 seconds end-to-end: 1-2s detection, <100ms classification, <500ms order submission.

Does this guarantee profits?

No. The strategy shows a statistically significant edge in out-of-sample testing, but markets evolve and past results don't guarantee future performance.

What if there are no announcements?

The bot waits. It only trades when a qualifying signal passes all checks. No forced trades.
