Strategy Logic

Simple thesis. Disciplined execution.

The strategy is grounded in a well-documented market inefficiency: Binance listing announcements create predictable short-term price impact on secondary exchanges.

01

Detect

Poll Binance announcement API every 1-2 seconds. Classify each post: is this a new listing signal for a coin already trading on MEXC?

02

Validate

Confirm the coin is actively trading on MEXC with sufficient liquidity. Filter out stablecoins, gold tokens, and mis-extracted symbols.

03

Execute

Submit a market order on MEXC within seconds. Position sized to 1% account risk, capped at 0.5% of pair volume.

04

Manage

Automated exit via take-profit (+13.6%), stop-loss, or 8-hour time stop. No manual intervention needed.

Spot Listing

Statistically significant pump on MEXC. Mean +76.6%, p < 0.001. This is the primary trade signal.

Launchpool / Launchpad

No statistically significant edge found. Excluded from trading after rigorous analysis.

Delisting

Used as a forced-exit signal only. If the bot holds a coin that gets delisted, it exits immediately.

Risk Management

Built to protect capital first.

Every trade has predefined risk limits. No exceptions, no overrides, no emotional decisions.

Position Sizing

1% account risk per trade. Position capped at 0.5% of pair's trailing volume.

Stop Loss

Every trade has a hard stop-loss. Calibrated from historical adverse excursion (30th percentile).

Time Stop

Positions auto-close after 8 hours. No holding overnight on momentum trades.

Daily Loss Limit

Trading halts if daily drawdown exceeds 5%. Resumes next UTC day automatically.

Concurrency Limit

Maximum 3 open positions at once. No more than 1 trade per asset.

Pessimistic Fills

Backtest uses worst-case convention: if TP and SL trigger in the same bar, SL is taken.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does this guarantee profits?

No. Past performance does not guarantee future results. The strategy shows a statistically significant edge in out-of-sample testing, but market conditions change and opportunities may decrease over time.

How fast does the bot react?

The target end-to-end latency is under 5 seconds: 1-2s for announcement detection, <100ms for classification, and <500ms for order submission on MEXC.

Why MEXC and not Binance for execution?

When Binance announces a new listing, the coin isn't tradeable on Binance yet — but it's often already listed on MEXC. We buy on MEXC to capture the anticipatory pump.

What happens if no opportunities appear?

The bot simply waits. It only trades when a qualifying announcement is detected and all validation checks pass. No forced trades, no overtrading.

Can I customize the strategy parameters?

The frozen strategy (M0) uses parameters calibrated from historical data. The dashboard shows all parameters transparently. Custom tuning is documented as future work.

Is my capital safe?

The bot uses trade-only API keys with no withdrawal permissions. Paper trading mode lets you validate everything before risking real capital. Daily loss limits and position caps provide additional protection.
